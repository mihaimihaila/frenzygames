What Makes a Good Jigsaw Puzzle Game on Windows?

A great free jigsaw puzzle game for Windows does more than scatter pieces across a screen. It earns daily play. The best ones get a few things right: the images are genuinely beautiful rather than stock-photo filler; piece sizes feel satisfying to handle with a mouse; and there's always something new to work toward — a fresh daily puzzle, a new pack, or a leaderboard position to improve. Equally important is depth without friction. Difficulty options (64 all the way to 400 pieces), tools like edge-only view and image peek, and a tray to keep loose pieces tidy — these small decisions separate games people keep installed from games they forget they downloaded.

Frenzy Games was built around exactly these priorities. Every game in the collection runs natively on Windows, feels polished on a large monitor, and gives you a free puzzle every single day without asking for a credit card.
