There are several ways to get crowns:

  1. You can convert coins to crowns. You get coins by solving puzzles and and by playing every day
  2. When you send a free puzzle gift to a friend you earn 30 crowns. Go to your Game account for detailed instructions
  3. When you create a game account you earn 10 crowns
  4. When you link a Facebook account to your game account you earn 5 crowns
  5. You can purchase crowns in your game account
