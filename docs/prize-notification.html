Prize Notification

Author: Mihai, last modified: 04/12/2024


Prizes are awarded when Puzzle of the Day finishes. The first 100 players in the leaderboard are awarded prizes.

To claim your prize follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Today page and look for the Prizes section
  2. Tap on the Claim button
  3. The reward is added to your account
