Why the Game contains Ads

Author: Mihai, last modified: 12/12/2020


The ads support the game and allow several puzzle packs and Puzzle of the Day to be free.

You can remove ads in several ways:

  • Purchase a Remove ads subscription
  • Purchase a Premium subscription to unlock the entire puzzle library and remove all ads
  • Purchase a puzzle pack to all puzzles in the pack and play them without any ads
