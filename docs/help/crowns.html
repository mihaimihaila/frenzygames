Crowns

Crowns are used for unlocking puzzles.

Crowns are earned:

  1. When you create a game account you earn crowns
  2. When you purchase puzzles with money you earn crowns
  3. When you complete missions you earn crowns
  4. When you recommended the game to a friend you earn crowns

Crowns can be purchased from the bank or by converting coins into crowns.

Convertion Rate

1 crown = 30.000 coins
