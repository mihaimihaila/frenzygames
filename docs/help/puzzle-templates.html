Puzzle Templates

Author: Mihai, last modified: 27/09/2025


The game uses several puzzle templates. These templates are custom made with different cuts to provide visual variety and enhance replayability. Each template defines how the puzzle pieces are shaped and interlocked, giving each puzzle a distinct look and feel.

Templates do not affect difficulty or image contentâ€”they only change how the pieces are arranged. Some cuts may appear more angular, rounded, or irregular, but all templates are balanced for general use.

By default, the game selects a template automatically for each puzzle. This rotation ensures that players encounter a mix of layouts over time, even when replaying the same image.

Notice

Players with an active Premium subscription can choose to use a fixed template in Settings. This disables automatic rotation and applies the same cut to all puzzles.

To turn off Dyanmic Templates:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Scroll to Premium
  3. Turn off Dynamic Templates.

Fixed templates are only available to Premium users. Free users will continue to experience dynamic rotation.
