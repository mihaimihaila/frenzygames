Gems

Gems are used for:

  • Playing Puzzle of the Day, costs 1 gem
  • Skipping ads, consts 30 gems
  • Refilling tools (Shuffle, Peek, Move to right, Move to left, Edges only)

Gems can be purchased from the bank or by converting coins into gems.

Convertion Rate

1 gem = 300 coins
