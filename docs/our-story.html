Our Story

Every time we get a chance, we spend endless hours playing board games. We have a cupboard full of games from scrabble, chess and backgammon to Settlers of Catan and Carcassonne.

Among other board games, we enjoy solving jigsaw puzzles in our spare time. However, we noticed that the search for the perfect puzzle image and the set-up logistics takes too long. After doing the same puzzle several times, we would get bored with it. We needed a better way to practice our hobby. We wanted a jigsaw puzzle which we would gladly come back to.

So, we decided to built a game that we could play every day without getting bored. Our plan was to implement a functionality to pick a new picture, the number of pieces and the shapes of the pieces before starting the puzzle.

That's how our first game, Jigsaw Puzzle Frenzy, was born. After that, we kept building games that we love playing.

We are incredible proud to be able to bring joy in peoples' lives every day.
