How Daily Puzzle Works

The daily puzzle is the same image for all players on that day — meaning everyone is solving the same puzzle, on the same clock. Your score is based purely on speed: how long it takes you to place the last piece.

1

Open the game

Download any Frenzy Games title for free. New players start with 300 gems — enough to jump straight in.

2

Tap Daily Puzzle

Find it in the main menu. It costs 3 gems to participate during the free trial period.

3

Choose your difficulty

Pick from 64, 100, 200, or 300 pieces — or 400 pieces with a Premium subscription.

4

Solve it fast

The timer starts when you place your first piece. Finish as quickly as you can to maximize your score.

5

See your rank

Your position on the global leaderboard updates in real time as other players finish.

6

Collect your coins

If you finish in the top 100, your prize appears in the Prizes panel on the home screen. Tap Claim to add the coins to your account.

Good to know: The puzzle can be saved and resumed any time while it's still active. If the daily window closes before you finish, the puzzle moves to your Saved pack so you can still complete it — though leaderboard rewards are only available while the competition is active.

Gems, Coins, Crowns — and Getting Started

Frenzy Games uses three currencies. Coins are earned through gameplay and daily rewards. Gems are used to enter game features like Daily Puzzle. Crowns are used to unlock puzzles, and can be earned through loyalty rewards, trading coins, or purchase.

Coins can be traded for gems or crowns, so everything connects: play regularly, accumulate coins, convert them to gems, and keep participating in the daily puzzle indefinitely.

Participating in Daily Puzzle costs gems. During the free trial period, it costs just 3 gems per day. Every new player receives 300 gems when they first install the game — enough to play the daily puzzle for months without spending a dime.

After the trial period ends, the participation cost increases to 100 gems per day. You have a few options: purchase gems directly, convert accumulated coins into gems, or subscribe to Premium for unlimited daily puzzle access.

New player tip: Your 300 starting gems are yours immediately. No tutorial to complete, no waiting period. Tap Daily Puzzle on day one and you're competing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the daily puzzle really free?

Yes. During the free trial period it costs 3 gems, and every new player starts with 300 gems. You can play for months without spending a dime.

When does the puzzle reset?

The puzzle resets at midnight. A new image — randomly selected from the game's full puzzle library — becomes available every day. You have 24 hours to complete each one.

Can I save and come back to it later?

Yes. Daily Puzzle can be saved and resumed at any time. If the daily window closes before you finish, the puzzle moves to your Saved pack so you can still complete it at your own pace — though leaderboard eligibility and coin rewards only apply while the competition window is open.

Do all four Frenzy Games titles have a daily puzzle?

Yes — Jigsaw Puzzle Quest, Jigsaw Puzzle Frenzy, Jigsaw Puzzle Journey, and Jigsaw Puzzle Nature all include Daily Puzzle with their own daily image and separate leaderboard.

What difficulty should I pick?

Any difficulty you like. Free players can choose from 64, 100, 200, or 300 pieces; 400 pieces is available with a Premium subscription. Lower piece counts solve faster and may give you an edge on the leaderboard; higher counts are more satisfying if you're not in a hurry.

How do I collect my coin prize?

After the competition closes, your final rank and prize appear in the Prizes panel on the home screen. Tap the Claim button to add the coins to your account — they won't be added automatically.

What can I do with coins?

Coins can be converted into gems or crowns. Gems are used to enter game features like Daily Puzzle. Crowns are used to unlock puzzles. Coins are earned through daily puzzle rewards, regular gameplay, and other in-game activities.

How is the daily puzzle image chosen?

Each day's puzzle is picked at random from the game's full library of thousands of images. You never know what you'll get — it could be a mountain landscape, a close-up of food, a city skyline, or a wildlife shot. That's part of the fun.
