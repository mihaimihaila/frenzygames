Configure Your Puzzle

Jigsaw Puzzle Journey can be played in several difficulty modes. Before starting a puzzle, pick the number of pieces. Jigsaw Puzzle Journey contains puzzles with: 64, 100, 200, 300 and 400 pieces.

Large Puzzle Library

Jigsaw Puzzle Journey contains over 1750 puzzles containing diverse images. The game contains packs of 25 puzzles each. We add new puzzle packs regurarly.

Tools to Help You Solve Puzzles

Jigsaw Puzzle Journey contains tools that help you solve the puzzles faster. You can peek at the entire image, move all pieces to the sides of the screen, shuffle the pieces or show the edge pieces only.

Participate in Competitions

Earn crowns by playing Puzzle of the Day. The faster you solve the puzzle, the higher your score. Crowns can be used to unlock new jigsaw puzzles.

A New Puzzle Every Day

Jigsaw Puzzle Journey contains a free puzzle every day. The puzzle is picked at random from our puzzle library and is unique each day. It can be played free of charge in any difficulty mode.
