New template

Mihai Mihaila, 12.02.2025

We have just launched a brand-new jigsaw puzzle template

Why This is a Big Deal: Many of you have been requesting fresh challenges, and after much anticipation, it is here! Our new template promises to keep even the most seasoned puzzle-solvers on their toes.

Thoughtful Update: The new template will be selected at random by the game, ensuring that every puzzle is a unique and challenging experience. The original template is still available, but you will never know which one you will get, adding an new layer of excitement to each game.

What to Expect:

Enhanced Gameplay: A whole new set of pieces and patterns to explore.

Increased Difficulty: Randomly selected templates mean no two puzzles are the same.

Classic Template: Available but also chosen at random to keep you guessing.
